About Us

Tired of paying loans and EMIs,then you have come to the right place.We provide cars for rent at reasonable rates.

All you need to do is make a security deposit and get behind the wheel.

We have a wide variety of cars ranging from hatchbacks and sedans to SUVs. Based on your location you can choose cars that are nearest to you.We also provide door step pickup points and drop points.

With simple and easy steps you can avail our cars at economical rates.We provide netbanking,credit,debit and paytm modes of payment. You will get an acknowledgement from our server about the booking and the exact location of the car will be specified along with the car details. You can also contact our customer service in case of any difficulties.

We have a secure and reliable system which enables locking and unlocking based on the user control

The current location and the distance travelled will be enabled at the time of your start. If you want extension of time, you can use extend ride option. New tariffs will come into effect.

In case of a car break down we will always be ready to help you. You can send your location and the assitance team will take care of it.
